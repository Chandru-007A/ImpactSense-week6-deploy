CDI

Community Weighted Intensity
A measure of the reported intensity of shaking as perceived by people in the affected area. It aggregates human feedback to gauge real-world impact.

MMI

Modified Mercalli Intensity
A scale used for measuring the intensity of shaking produced by an earthquake. Unlike magnitude, intensity varies based on location and surface geology.

MAGNITUDE

Seismic Energy Release
A logarithmic measure of the energy released by an earthquake at its source. Each whole number increase represents a 10x increase in amplitude.

DEPTH

Hypocenter Verticality
The distance from the earth's surface to the point where the earthquake originated. Shallower quakes often cause more surface damage.

SIGNIFICANCE

Event Impact Score
A composite value (0-3000) representing the overall impact of an earthquake, calculated based on magnitude, intensity, and reported effects.
